# Obscura Academy

Obscura Academy is an interactive, educational platform designed to teach cryptography, puzzle-solving, and digital forensics. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned sleuth, the academy guides you through essential skills used in internet ARGs (Alternate Reality Games), cryptography, steganography, and more. Our mission is to empower learners to uncover hidden messages, crack codes, and explore the world of digital mysteries.

## Features

- **Hands-on Cryptography Lessons**: Start with simple ciphers like Caesar and Vigenère, and progress to more advanced encryption techniques.
- **Puzzle Challenges**: Apply your skills to solve increasingly complex cryptographic puzzles and mystery cases.
- **Interactive Tools**: Practice encryption, decryption, and cryptographic analysis with built-in tools to decode messages.
- **Progressive Learning**: Courses and modules designed to build knowledge from foundational cryptography to more advanced digital forensics and ARG skills.
- **Engaging Cases**: Solve real-world inspired mysteries while learning the art of cryptography and code-breaking.
